About Us

Welcome to Al Noor Farm

Located in Multan, the global heartland of the finest mangoes, Al Noor Farm has been cultivating premium, export-grade mangoes with dedication, passion, and precision.

We believe that the king of fruits deserves nothing short of royal treatment. Our family-owned orchards maintain standard agricultural practices to nurture each tree. We select only the top 5% of our harvest for direct-to-consumer boxes under the Al Noor Farm brand, ensuring you get the size, aroma, and taste that Pakistani mangoes are world-famous for.

Quality Standards

Hygienic Hot Water Treatment

At Al Noor Farm, we follow export-grade post-harvest protocols. We subject our mangoes to a specialized Hot Water Treatment (HWT).

This eco-friendly treatment (keeping mangoes at exactly 48°C for a specified time) effectively eliminates any insect larvae, pathogens, or fruit flies without the use of chemical pesticides. Furthermore, this process balances the fruit's natural enzymes, enhancing uniform ripening, locking in sweetness, and extending the shelf life by 4-5 days, ensuring they reach your home in perfect condition.
